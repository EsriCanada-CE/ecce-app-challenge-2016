Surface Area

Eligible surface area is the most important factor for solar panels. With more available roof space, more panels can be installed, and more energy can be generated. Our calculations estimate that you will utilize 35% of your roof area, change this value as you wish to influence the ouput.

Panel Direction Index

For optimum performance of your panels, they must be facing due South. SolarSaver has calculated a Direction Index for every building in the Halifax Area. This index is a weighted average of the directions each specific section of your roof are facing. If you plan to have all your panels facing a certain direction change the value with the index drop-down menu and your energy generation and saving will automatically adjust.

Pitch of Roof

The optimal angle for solar panels to be positioned is generally equal to the latitude of your house. For Halifax, this would be about 45 degrees. Slight changes to this value won't impact your efficiency too much, but major changes (greater than 15 degrees) will have a profound effect.

Shading

Shade is caused by objects blocking the sunlight, and will therefore negatively impact your panels' performance. Ideally, panels should not be shaded at all.

Snow Cover

Living in Canada, we are not unfamiliar with excess snowfall. Snow covering your panels won't allow for sunlight to reach them, and will therefore decrease your annual returns. Don't forget to clean them off in the winter!

New to Solar?

Save on your Power Bill

One of the biggest advantages of solar energy is immediate electricity bill savings. Through these savings, you will start to see a return on your investment, and a decrease in your energy bill.

Incentives, Incentives, Incentives!

There are several programs and incentives such as tax-free grants to kickstart your solar journey. View the Solar Nova Scotia and Halifax Solar City links below to explore your options!

Reduce your Carbon Footprint

Replace your utility power with clean, renewable, and sustainable electricity from solar panels. Every little bit helps in the transition to a carbon-free lifestyle.

Increase your Home Value

Solar panels translate into lower electricity costs, increasing your home's attractiveness on the market. A study conducted by the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) found that homes with solar panels sell 20% faster, and at 17% higher values, than those without.
